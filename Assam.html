Bihu

Bihu is the most significant festival in Assam, celebrated in three forms: Rongali Bihu (April), Bohag Bihu (mid-April, marking the Assamese New Year), and Magh Bihu (January). The festival marks the start of the harvest season, and it involves traditional dances, songs, feasts, and the famous "Bihu dance."

Eid-ul-Fitr

Eid-ul-Fitr, celebrated by the Muslim community in Assam, marks the end of Ramadan. People offer prayers at mosques, exchange gifts, and feast with family and friends. The occasion is marked by communal harmony, with people of all faiths participating in the celebrations.
