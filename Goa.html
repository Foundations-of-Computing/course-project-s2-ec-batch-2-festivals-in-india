Goa

Carnival

Carnival, celebrated in February, is one of the most vibrant and grand festivals in Goa. It marks the beginning of Lent in the Christian calendar, but it is celebrated with parades, music, dance, floats, and street performances. People enjoy a lively atmosphere with a mix of Portuguese and Goan traditions.

Shigmo

Shigmo, celebrated in March, is the Goan version of Holi and is a colorful festival marking the arrival of spring. It includes grand parades, folk dances, music, and vibrant processions in various parts of Goa. The festival celebrates the rich cultural diversity of the region.

Ganesh Chaturthi

Ganesh Chaturthi, celebrated in August or September, is a widely observed festival in Goa. It marks the birth of Lord Ganesha, and the festival is known for its elaborate processions, music, dance, and the immersion of Ganesha idols in rivers after the celebration.
