Madhya pradesh

Diwali

Diwali is celebrated in October or November with grandeur across the state. Homes and temples are illuminated with lamps, and fireworks light up the night sky.

Holi

Holi falls in March and is celebrated with enthusiasm in cities like Indore and Ujjain. People enjoy playing with colors, singing, and dancing to welcome the arrival of spring.

Bhagoria Festival

Bhagoria Festival is celebrated in March, just before Holi, by the Bhil and Bhilala tribes. It is famous for its fairs, traditional tribal music, and a unique custom where young men and women choose their life partners.
