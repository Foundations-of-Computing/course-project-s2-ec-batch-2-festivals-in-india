Maharashtra

Ganesh Chaturthi

Ganesh Chaturthi is the most popular festival in Maharashtra, celebrated in August or September. Large, beautifully crafted idols of Lord Ganesha are worshipped in homes and public pandals, followed by grand processions for immersion (Visarjan). Mumbai and Pune host some of the most spectacular celebrations.

Navratri & Durga Puja

Navratri is observed in September or October with nine days of devotional fasting, Garba, and Dandiya Raas. Durga Puja is also celebrated with grandeur in Mumbai, particularly in Bengali communities.

Makar Sankranti

Makar Sankranti, celebrated on January 14, marks the transition of the sun into Capricorn. People fly kites, exchange "tilgul" (sesame and jaggery sweets), and greet each other with "Tilgul ghya, god god bola" (Take sweets, speak sweetly).
