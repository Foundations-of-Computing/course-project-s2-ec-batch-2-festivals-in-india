Meghalaya

Sohra Festival

Celebrated in the months of April and May. This is a celebration of the cultural heritage of the Khasi tribe. It includes traditional dances, music, and various competitions. The festival aims to showcase the rich culture and traditions of the people of Meghalaya.

Nongkrem Festival

Usually held in November.This is one of the most important festivals of the Khasi tribe. It is a harvest festival dedicated to the deity "Sohra," and involves dancing, rituals, and offerings. The festival is marked by the famous "Nongkrem dance," performed by both men and women in traditional attire.

Durga Puja

September or October, depending on the lunar calendar.Although Durga Puja is primarily celebrated by the Bengali community, it is also a major festival for many other communities in Meghalaya. The festival honors the goddess Durga and involves elaborate rituals, processions, and cultural performances.

Eid al-Fitr

Celebrated in July.This festival is one of the largest festivals of the Jaintia tribe. It is a harvest festival that marks the end of the sowing season and prays for a good harvest. The most prominent event is the "Behdienkhlam dance," accompanied by the beating of drums and traditional songs.
