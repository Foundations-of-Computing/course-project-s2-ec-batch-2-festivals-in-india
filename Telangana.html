Telangana

Bathukamma

Bathukamma is a floral festival celebrated mainly by women in Telangana during September or October. Women create colorful flower arrangements in a cone shape, sing folk songs, and immerse them in water bodies, praying for prosperity and well-being.

Bonalu

Bonalu, celebrated in July or August, is a major folk festival dedicated to Goddess Mahakali. Women prepare offerings of rice cooked with milk and jaggery in decorated pots and offer them to the goddess in temples, especially in Hyderabad and Secunderabad.

Nagoba Jatara

Nagoba Jatara is a tribal festival celebrated by the Gond community in Adilabad district in January. It is dedicated to their serpent deity, Nagoba, and features traditional rituals, processions, and cultural performances.
