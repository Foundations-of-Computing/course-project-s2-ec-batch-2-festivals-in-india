Tripura

Kharchi Puja

Kharchi Puja is one of the most important festivals of Tripura, celebrated in July at the Chaturdasha Temple in Agartala. It is dedicated to the 14 deities of the royal Manikya dynasty and involves rituals, processions, and community feasts.

Garia Puja

Garia Puja, held in April, is a major tribal festival dedicated to Baba Garia, the deity of livestock and wealth. The indigenous communities offer prayers, sacrifice cocks, and perform traditional dances to seek blessings for prosperity.
