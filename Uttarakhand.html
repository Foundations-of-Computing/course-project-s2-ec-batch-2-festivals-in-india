Uttarakhand

Basant Panchami

Basant Panchami, celebrated in February, marks the arrival of spring. It is dedicated to Goddess Saraswati, and people fly kites and wear yellow-colored clothes to celebrate the occasion.

Kumbh Mela & Ardh Kumbh Mela

Kumbh Mela, held every 12 years in Haridwar, is one of the largest religious gatherings in the world. Devotees take a holy dip in the Ganges River to cleanse their sins. Ardh Kumbh Mela is celebrated every six years in Haridwar.

Phool Dei

Phool Dei, celebrated in March, is a harvest festival where young girls decorate houses with fresh flowers, sing folk songs, and bless families with prosperity.
