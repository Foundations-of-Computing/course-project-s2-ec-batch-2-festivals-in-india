Losar

Losar, celebrated in February or March, is the Tibetan New Year and is one of the most important festivals in Arunachal Pradesh, especially among the Monpa, Sherdukpen, and other Tibetan-influenced communities. The festival involves traditional dances, music, feasting, and the lighting of lamps to usher in the new year.

Taman Ngaye

Taman Ngaye is celebrated by the Adi tribe in December. It marks the harvest season and is an occasion for thanksgiving to deities for a bountiful crop. The celebrations include ritual dances, feasts, and prayers for prosperity.

Solung Festival

Solung Festival, celebrated in September, is an important agricultural festival for the Adi tribe. The festival is marked by elaborate rituals to please the deities, followed by folk dances, community feasts, and animal sacrifices in some regions.
