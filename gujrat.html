Gujarat

Navratri

Navratri, celebrated in September or October, is the most famous and vibrant festival of Gujarat. It is dedicated to Goddess Durga and lasts for nine nights, featuring the traditional Garba and Dandiya Raas dances. People dress in colorful attire and gather in large groups to dance to the rhythmic beats of folk music. The festival is a grand cultural spectacle, attracting tourists from across the world.

Uttarayan (International Kite Festival)

Uttarayan, celebrated on January 14, marks Makar Sankranti, the transition of the sun into Capricorn. It is popularly known as the Kite Festival, where the skies of Gujarat, especially in cities like Ahmedabad, are filled with colorful kites. People engage in friendly kite-flying competitions, and the festival is accompanied by delicious sweets like "Undhiyu" and "Chikki."
